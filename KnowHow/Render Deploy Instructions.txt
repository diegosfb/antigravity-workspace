Render Deploy Instructions


1) Go to Render.com and sign in with your GitHub account.
2) Click the "New" button in the top right.
3) Select "Blueprint" (this is important, it tells Render to read the render.yaml file I created!).
4) Connect the BetterTris-v2 repository.
5) For A unique name for your Blueprint, you can type whatever you want! I recommend something like BattleTris-Server
For The path to the Blueprint file in your repo, because I placed render.yaml in the main root folder
6) Render will detect the configuration automatically and start building your app.
7) Check your Render dashboard; within a minute or two, they will provide you with a live https://battletris-server.onrender.com URL that you and your friends can use to play from anywhere!

Once the deployment finishes building and goes live, Render automatically generates a public URL for your game!

You can find the link right on your Render Dashboard:

Go to your Render dashboard and click on the new battletris-server Web Service.
In the top-left corner (right under the service name), you will see a URL that looks something like https://battletris-server.onrender.com (it might have a few random letters/numbers added to the end).
Click that link—that is the exact, permanent public URL you can share with anyone on the internet to play your game!
